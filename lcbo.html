LCBO

LCBO is an Ontario government enterprise and one of the world’s largest buyers and retailers of beverage alcohol. Through more than 634 retail stores and catalogues, it offers nearly 18,000 products annually to consumers and licensed establishments. In fiscal 2012-13, LCBO sales topped $4.892 billion and it delivered a $1.7 billion dividend to the Ontario government, not including taxes.
